R3

A framework to align your team’s vision, focus, and execution for maximum impact.

Vision

Clarify your team’s long-term purpose and direction. Define the impact you want to create and the future you’re striving to build.

Traction

Create the structure, focus, and accountability to turn your vision into reality. Ensure consistent progress and team alignment.

Action

Set, track, and measure your team’s top quarterly priorities. Break down goals into clear tasks, assign ownership, and monitor progress to ensure tangible results.
